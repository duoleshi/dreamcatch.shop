How to Play

  1. 1

    Objective

    The goal is to uncover all 13 safe cells without clicking on any of the 3 hidden mines. Use logic and deduction to determine where the mines are located.

  2. 2

    Game Mechanics

    Left-click on cells to reveal what's underneath. If you click on a mine, the game ends. If you click on a safe cell, a number appears showing how many mines are in the adjacent cells.

  3. 3

    Reading the Numbers

    Numbers indicate how many mines are in the surrounding 8 cells (fewer for edge cells). Use these numbers to make logical deductions about where mines are located.

  4. 4

    Winning the Game

    Successfully reveal all cells except those containing mines to win. The challenge is to use the numerical clues to safely navigate the grid.

About DreamCatch

DreamCatch is a modern take on the classic minesweeper game, designed to provide a quick yet challenging experience. Our 4x4 grid format offers a perfect balance between accessibility and difficulty, making it suitable for both casual players and puzzle enthusiasts.

The game features three randomly placed mines across a 16-cell grid, creating a different challenge each time you play. As you reveal safe cells, numbers appear indicating how many mines are in the adjacent cells, helping you make informed decisions about where to click next.
