How to Play

  1. 1

    Game Objective

    Your goal is to uncover all 97 safe cells without clicking on any of the 3 hidden mines. Use numbers as clues to deduce where mines are located.

  2. 2

    Cell Interaction

    Left-click on a cell to reveal what's underneath. If it's a mine, the game ends. If it's safe, a number appears showing how many mines are in the adjacent cells.

  3. 3

    Understanding Numbers

    The numbers indicate how many mines are in the surrounding eight cells (fewer for edge cells). For example, if a cell shows "2", that means two of the adjacent cells contain mines.

  4. 4

    Winning Strategy

    Use logic to determine which cells are safe to click. When all safe cells are revealed, you win! The key is to make educated guesses based on the number clues provided.
