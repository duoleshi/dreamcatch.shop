DreamCatch: Challenge Your Mind with Minesweeper

A thrilling 4x4 minesweeper game that tests your logic and decision-making skills. Can you find all safe cells while avoiding the hidden mines?

Game Features

Simple 4x4 Grid

A compact 4x4 grid makes it perfect for quick gaming sessions while still providing a challenging experience.

Strategic Mine Placement

Three mines are randomly distributed across the grid, creating a unique challenge each time you play.

Logical Gameplay

Numbers indicate adjacent mines, helping you make logical deductions to safely clear the board.

How to Play

  1. 1

    Start the Game

    Click on any cell to begin. Choose wisely - your first move could reveal a mine!

  2. 2

    Read the Numbers

    When you click on a safe cell, a number appears indicating how many mines are in the adjacent cells. Use this information to deduce where mines are located.

  3. 3

    Avoid Mines

    If you click on a cell containing a mine, the game is over. Be careful with your selections!

  4. 4

    Win the Game

    Successfully reveal all 13 safe cells (all cells that don't contain mines) to win the game. The challenge is to do this without clicking on any of the 3 hidden mines.

About DreamCatch

DreamCatch is a modern twist on the classic minesweeper game, designed to provide a quick but challenging gaming experience. With a compact 4x4 grid and three strategically placed mines, each game offers a perfect balance of luck and skill.

This game is perfect for players who enjoy logical puzzles and want to test their deductive reasoning. The simple interface makes it accessible for players of all ages, while still providing enough challenge to keep experienced gamers engaged.

Whether you're looking to kill a few minutes during a break or wanting to exercise your brain with a quick logic puzzle, DreamCatch provides the perfect combination of simplicity and challenge.
